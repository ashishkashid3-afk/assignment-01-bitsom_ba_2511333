## Architecture Recommendation
I recommend Data Lakehouse Architecture
1st reason-In this case we have to dealt with structured, semi-structured and unstructured datahouse, so for this Data lakehouse used as single platform.
2nd reason-Data Lakehouse provides uniqueness,scalability, performance & reliability to support analysis by ensuring data quality and consistency.
3rd reason-Real time data such as customer reviews & GPS logs and batch processing data such as payment transactions handled efficiently.
